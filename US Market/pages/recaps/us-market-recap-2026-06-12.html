Fed & Policy Setup

Hold next week remains the base case, but the year-end policy distribution is still hawkish.

Next FOMC

June 16-17, 2026

The immediate event risk for equities, rates, and small caps.

Base Case

Hold

No change expected next week, but communication risk is high under the new chair.

2026 Repricing

Hawkish skew

Barron’s cited 59.6% odds of at least one hike later in 2026, only 1.4% odds of cuts this year.

Macro interpretation

The market is not pricing recession. It is pricing a resilient growth backdrop with inflation still sticky enough to keep the Fed from easing. That is why small caps can rally tactically while duration stays under pressure.
