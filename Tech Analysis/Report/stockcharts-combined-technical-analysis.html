Bottom Line

Taken together, the five lists describe a market that is still in an uptrend and broadening below the surface, but with clear rotation and a few soft spots. The healthiest evidence is broad participation: expanding new highs, contained new lows, and leadership from the Dow, NYSE Composite, Value Line, small caps, and cyclical sectors like Industrials, Financials, and Health Care.

The recurring caution is Nasdaq and large-cap growth. Nasdaq lags on price, daily breadth, and tactical timing, while Communication Services, Energy, and Consumer Discretionary drag on sector breadth. Sentiment is supportive but not washed out — positioning is crowded and volatility is calm, so there is little cushion if the tape turns.
