What is a Bullish Percent Index? Each BPI shows the percentage of stocks in an index currently on a point-and-figure buy signal. Above 50 means most stocks are in uptrends (a bullish majority); readings above 70 are considered overbought and below 30 oversold. Reading several indexes side by side reveals whether a rally is broad or narrow.
